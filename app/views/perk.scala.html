SproutUp Reward Program

We want SproutUp experience to be awesome and rewarding, so we are stoked to launch a program for you to earn Reward Points. Excited? See how it works!

Create content

After your product trial, earn points by posting blogs, YouTube or Vine videos, or Periscope live demos on SproutUp Buzz. Shoutouts on social media count, too!

We measure the performance of your content. The more views your content drives, the more points you earn. Make sure in "Profiles & Settings", you authorize SproutUp to read data from you Google or YouTube analytics.
