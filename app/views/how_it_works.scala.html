A short story

We love emerging products - IoT gadgets, tech toys, wearable devices, smart monitors and anything innovative. About a year ago, we got totally jazzed about helping creators successfully bring rad products to the market. Kudos to crowdfunding platforms – Kickstarter, Indiegogo et al. that help fund innovation and provide pre-orders!! What’s next? Spreading awareness on these innovations among secondary adopters and scaling the user base.

While big brands have the resources to do this easily, there is a better way for up-and-coming brands: collaborating with a network of passionate social influencers to expand outreach and drive sales. The fun part for influencers is to discover innovative products, try those they are genuinely interested in, create awesome content, and spread the word.

How it works

1

Request a product for trial

Product Trials provide influencers that have passion for technology with early access to exciting, cutting-edge and rad products. We love awesome YouTubers, bloggers, journalists/writers or enthusiasts with a large following on Twitter, Instagram or Facebook. SproutUp offers a choice model that allows you to place up to 3 active trial requests on any products you love from our product catalog.

2

Try product and generate experience

SproutUp ships one product out of your three active requests, along with trial instructions, to your home. If the product excites you, capture your authentic experience in a compelling visual content. Content could be in the form of a short YouTube video, a blog/media article with photos, or a live demo via Periscope. Ideally, a trial runs for two to three weeks after you receive the product. We provide you with a shipping label to return the product. Once a product is returned, we mail you next available product from your active request list.

3

Community-oriented content promotion

Promote your content through social media so consumers get genuine and entertaining information on product’s utility. We create a unique URL for you to embed in your content that allows us to track referred visits and conversions on SproutUp. Encourage your followers to lend their network to share your content. We amplify the outreach using our social media channels and collectively use the content of 3-5 influencers that participated in product trials. The result is a super large outreach in front of targeted audience.

4

Earn perks

SproutUp is based on perks-for-performance model. For each trial, we track activities and outcomes. Perks are both monetary and non-monetary in nature. You not only earn perks for your individual performance, but also based on the team’s performance. Additionally, trial participation helps expand your influence circle.
