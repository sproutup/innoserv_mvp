How it started

SproutUp's concept was seeded at MIT's Muddy Charles Pub over a pitcher of IPA when Nitin first noticed the struggle of creators of emerging prodcuts to commercialize. It took the Fall 2014 to evolve the concept further and turn the idea into a startup when Nitin and Tao partnered up as co-founders of SproutUp.

Who we are

We are fun-loving individuals who came together aiming to develop a deeper connection between creators of emerging product and product enthusiasts and consumers.

Nitin Jain

CEO & co-founder. MIT Sloan MBA. Ex-executive at IBM and serial technology entrepreneur.

Tao Ni

CPO & co-founder. PhD, Virginia Tech. Ex-LivingSocial, MicroStrategy and Microsoft Research.

Peter Andersen

Director of Engineering. Serial technology entrepreneur. MSc, Tech University of Denmark.

Why we are doing it

Deeply inspired by the lessons of "The Lean Startup" by Eric Ries, we believe that "Build, Measure, Learn, and Grow" approach can help emerging product creators better understand their customers, measure product usability, and discover new ideas for further product innovation.
