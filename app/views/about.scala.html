Our roots

The SproutUp concept was seeded at MIT’s Muddy Charles Pub where Nitin often heard the struggle of product creators to move past the first stage of product development. For example, one creator said "Dude – it's harder than I thought to really know what my customers want." The other inventor commented that "I have no meaningful way to collaborate with my early adopters except painstakingly stalking them on Facebook." Seeing this as a common problem across several creators, Nitin and Tao quit their day jobs in Fall 2014 to found SproutUp. And here we are – launching SproutUp Beta.

We have a mission

We believe that the best products are never created in a box. Our goal is to help product creators further their innovation to deliver the best products in the market. We want to achieve this by developing a community of co-creators - product creators, early adopters and product enthusiast.
