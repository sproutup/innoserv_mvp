Our roots

The SproutUp concept was seeded at MIT’s Muddy Charles Pub over a pitcher of IPA where Nitin was chatting with a friend and product creator about how hard it was to move past the first stage of product development. "Dude – this is harder than I thought," said the product creator. "I have no way to collaborate with people who have actually used my product except painstakingly stalking them on Facebook." Nitin and Tao quit their day jobs in Fall 2014 to found SproutUp. And here we are – launching our beta. The future belongs to you.

We have a mission

We believe that the best ideas are never discovered in a box. Our goal is to develop a community of co-creators - where product creators have the opportunity to publicly collaborate with early adapters and product enthusiasts.
