What is Buzz

Buzz is a place for you, influencers, to showcase the awesome content you create for emerging products.

Benefits of Buzz

Show off your talent

You've got talent on creating intriguing content - time to show off to the world!

Get broad distribution

Your content will be promoted to a broad community of tech influencers and enthusiasts, in addition to your existing channels.

Socialize with others

Connect with like-minded tech enthusiasts, upvote or share content you love, and make constructive comments to help each other grow.

Earn rewards

Earn rewards for publishing great content and actively participating in quality discussions.

What to post

We love original content about your product trial experience, or questions and discussions about topics relevant to emerging products. We love all kinds of content: articles, photos, short or long videos.

You may publish by embedding a URL, uploading photo or video files, or writing directly on SproutUp (coming soon).

We highly appreciate quality comments on other's content. Please avoid bashing or ranting on products or people.
