Why should I link my social media accounts with SproutUp?
  • Verify. Connecting accounts helps us make sure you're a the real account owner. 😎
  • Qualify. We use your collective social media reach to determine qualification for product trials.
  • Measure. Get bragging rights when you move up the rank on SproutUp leader board.
  • Earn. Earn Sprout Points based on your content’s performance.
  • Assurance. Don’t worry, we’ll never post on your accounts unless you tell us to.
